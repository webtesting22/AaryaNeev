COMPANY - HISTORY

In the vibrant city of Ahmedabad, nestled within the heart of Gujarat, a remarkable tale of determination and perseverance unfolded during the 19th century. Amidst the challenges and struggles of that era, a man named Mr. Ratilal Patel found himself laboring diligently within the confines of the Cimplex Mill. The mill itself was no stranger to adversity, facing its own battles for survival. Yet, within these trying times, Mr. Ratilal Patel emerged as a beacon of resilience.

As the sole breadwinner for his family, Mr. Patel's responsibilities extended far beyond the walls of the mill. While he dedicated his days to the challenges of the mill, he also embarked on a second journey as the proprietor of an ironing shop. This dual pursuit showcased his unwavering commitment to securing a better future for his loved ones.

With unwavering ambition and an unyielding spirit, Mr. Patel's endeavors flourished. He gradually formed partnerships with four like-minded individuals who shared his vision for progress. Together, they hatched a plan to establish a small business that would serve their colony society. This endeavor came to fruition on a plot of land owned by Mr. Patel in 1989.

Upon successfully completing their initial project, Mr. Patel's aspirations knew no bounds. In 1990, he took a bold leap into the world of construction, birthing a venture that would eventually be known as "AARYAVRAT DEVELOPERS." Teaming up with four other families, this newly-formed alliance set forth to transform their dreams into tangible structures.

AARYAVRAT DEVELOPERS embarked on a journey that defied the odds. Through diligence, determination, and a shared commitment to excellence, the company ascended to heights that surpassed even their wildest aspirations. The visionary efforts of Mr. Ratilal Patel and his partners captured the imagination of the community, elevating them to the status of business tycoons.

As time flowed like a river, the baton of leadership seamlessly transitioned. Mr. Ratilal Patel's legacy lived on through his son, Shailesh R. Patel, who took the reins of the thriving empire. Under Shailesh's guidance, the company continued to thrive, embracing innovation and expansion. The luxuries of contemporary living became woven into the fabric of AARYAVRAT DEVELOPERS' ongoing projectsâ€”elegant flats, majestic bungalows, and inviting weekend villas.

Today, the name AARYAVRAT DEVELOPERS resonates as a symbol of excellence, a testament to the indomitable spirit of Mr. Ratilal Baldevbhai Patel and his family. Their journey, which began in the confines of a struggling mill and an ironing shop, has blossomed into an enduring legacy. The once-humble aspirations of a man and his partners have flourished into a renowned company that continues to shape the skyline of Ahmedabad and the dreams of generations to come.
